Abhisarga 2025

Banner Submission Guidelines

Dear Technical Clubs,

As part of Abhisarga 2025, every technical club is required to submit a corresponding Banner for their club and its event. Please ensure that your submission meets the following requirements:

Banner Submission Requirements

Institute Details:

• Institute logo

Club Details:

• Club logo

• Club name

Event Details:

• Event name matching the fest's theme

• Relevant content

Design Theme:

• Reflect the fest’s theme: Mystery and Magic

Size:

• 7 × 4

Note

Please leave some space on your banner for the addition of sponsor details, which will be added by the Design Team.

Poster Submission Reminder

A reminder for the poster submission (details sent on December 10th, 2024). The deadline for the poster submission is 31st January 2025.

Banner submission date will be declared later.

Let’s make Abhisarga a magical and memorable event!
